What this contract can never do

The entire contract is 15 lines on top of OpenZeppelin's audited ERC-20. The full 10 billion supply was minted once, in the constructor — and there is no owner and not a single admin function. These aren't promises; they're absences of code, verifiable by anyone.

🚫

No minting

There is no mint function. The supply is 10,000,000,000 NXIO today, and it will be 10,000,000,000 NXIO in fifty years.

❄️

No pausing

Transfers can never be frozen — not for everyone, not for anyone. The contract has no pause switch to flip.

🔓

No blacklist

No address can ever be blocked from sending or receiving. There is no list, and no one with the keys to keep one.

💸

No fees or taxes

Send 100 NXIO, and 100 NXIO arrives. No transfer tax, no reflections, no hidden skim — ever.

🧊

No upgrades

Not a proxy. The bytecode on-chain today is final — no one can swap in different behavior later.

👤

No owner

The deploying wallet kept 0 NXIO and zero special powers. There is no privileged address at all.

Add NXIO to your wallet

NXIO is a standard ERC-20 — any wallet that speaks Base can hold it.

Switch your wallet's network to Base (chain ID 8453). It's built into MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Rabby and most others.

Choose Import token → custom token, and paste the official contract address:
